Red Lentil Curry

time: 40 min servings: 4

    Ingredients

    First

  • 1 cup red lentils
  • 1/2 large onion, diced

    • Second: spices

  • 1.5 tsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp red curry paste
  • 1.5 tsp curry powder
  • .5 tsp ground tumeric
  • .5 tsp ground cumin
  • .5 tsp chili powder
  • .5 tsp sugar
  • .5 tsp minced garlic
  • .5 tsp fresh ginger, minced

    • Final

  • 8 oz tomato purée

    Directions

  1. Wash the lentils in cold water until the water runs clear. Put lentils in a pot with enough water to cover; bring to a boil, place a cover on the pot, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer, adding water during cooking as needed to keep covered, until tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onions in hot oil until caramelized, about 20 minutes.
  3. Stir spices into the onions. Increase heat to high and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Stir the tomato puree into the spices, remove from heat and stir into the lentils.

Ratatouille

time: 1h servings: 4

    Ingredients

    Chop/Sautee First

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp dried parseley
  • 1 medium eggplant, 1cm cubes

    • Chop/Layer second

  • 1 large onion, sliced into rings
  • 2 medium zucchini,
  • 2 cups mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 whole green bell pepper, sliced

    • Add Last

  • to taste salt
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced or diced

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Coat bottom and sides of a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish with 1 tablespoon olive oil.
  2. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Cook and stir garlic until lightly browned. Mix in parsley and eggplant. Cook and stir until eggplant is soft, about 10 minutes. Season with salt to taste.
  3. Spread eggplant mixture evenly across bottom of prepared casserole dish. Sprinkle with a few tablespoons of Parmesan cheese. Spread zucchini in an even layer over top. Lightly salt and sprinkle with a little more cheese. Continue layering in this fashion, with onion, mushrooms, bell pepper, and tomatoes, covering each layer with a sprinkling of salt and cheese.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 45 minutes.

Vegetarian "Bibimbap"

time:50m 3

    Ingredients

    Stir Fry

  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup carrot matchsticks
  • 1 cup zucchini matchsticks
  • 1 cup packed bean sprouts
  • 6 oz ounces canned bamboo shoots, drained
  • .5 cup packed sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup sliced green onions
  • to taste salt
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

    • Other stuff

  • 2 cups cooked and cooled rice
  • 1 tbsp butter/oil
  • 3 eggs
  • .25 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 tsp sweet red chili sauce , or to taste

    Directions

  1. Chop vegetables into thin sticks.
  2. Stir fry vegetables. Cook rice for 20min
  3. Stir cooked rice, soy sauce, and black pepper in the same skillet until the rice is hot. In a separate skillet over medium heat, melt butter and gently fry eggs, turning once, until the yolks are still slightly runny but the egg whites are firm, about 3 minutes per egg. [see Fried Eggs if you do not know how to fry eggs.]
  4. divide servings, top rice with eggs and stir fry. Add hot sauce to taste.
